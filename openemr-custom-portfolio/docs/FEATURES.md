# Features Overview

## POS Enhancements

- Modal-driven checkout workflow embedded into clinic operations
- Support for consult, medicine, dispense, and administer flows
- Transaction history visibility for front-desk and billing staff
- Receipt rendering improvements for operational clarity
- Backdated POS transaction support for correction workflows
- Approval and override handling for sensitive billing scenarios

## Inventory Enhancements

- Quantity-on-hand visibility during sales and medication handling
- Inventory deduction linked to dispense and administer actions
- Lot-aware inventory behavior
- Product-specific inventory rules and safeguards

## Reporting Enhancements

- Enhanced Daily Collection Report logic
- Patient-level and medicine-level reporting views
- Better operational visibility into payment and transaction activity
- Facility-aware filters and reporting support
- Export-oriented reporting refinements

## Finder and Workflow Enhancements

- Custom patient finder behavior for clinic staff
- Facility-aware filtering logic
- Payment and balance visibility in the finder workflow
- Faster navigation into POS and patient actions
